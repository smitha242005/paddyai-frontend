About PaddyAI

Our mission, technology stack, and the team behind the platform

Our Mission

PaddyAI was built to bridge the gap between modern artificial intelligence and traditional paddy farming. Millions of smallholder farmers in India and Southeast Asia face losses each season due to undetected crop diseases, poor soil management, and inefficient water usage.

We harness state-of-the-art computer vision AI to give every farmer — whether they own 1 acre or 100 acres — access to the same expert agronomic advice that large agribusinesses receive. Instantly. For free.
